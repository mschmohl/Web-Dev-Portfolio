Information About Me

I see the world through a different lens and inspiring creativity excites me! Beginning my career as a Graphic Designer I first discovered my passion for creating aesthetically appealing designs and concepts.

Art has always been the backbone of my career and after completing my BA in Fine Arts I decided to attain my Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education. This allowed me to enter the education sector where I could inspire young learners not only through art but through technology.

Constantly pursuing self-development in my career I am always remaining current on the latest trends and developments related to not only education and art but graphic and web design as well.
